Actually this operator is kind of {\ttfamily concat(obs, obs...)} where obs repeated {\ttfamily count} times

\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\ttfamily repeat(0)} -\/ means no any subscription at all
\item {\ttfamily repeat(1)} -\/ behave like ordinal observable
\item {\ttfamily repeat(10)} -\/ 1 normal subscription and 9 re-\/subscriptions during {\ttfamily on\+\_\+completed}
