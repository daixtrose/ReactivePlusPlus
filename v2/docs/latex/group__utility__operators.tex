Re-\/subscribes on current observable provided amount of times when {\ttfamily on\+\_\+completed} obtained. 



Actually this operator does concat(obs, obs, obs....)


\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em count} & total amount of times subscription happens. For example\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\ttfamily repeat(0)} -\/ means no any subscription at all
\item {\ttfamily repeat(1)} -\/ behave like ordinal observable
\item {\ttfamily repeat(10)} -\/ 1 normal subscription and 9 re-\/subscriptions during on\+\_\+completed 
\end{DoxyItemize}\\
